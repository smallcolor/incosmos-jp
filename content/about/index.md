## Company Identity

InCosmos · 在宇 (ZAIU Inc.) is an Osaka-based innovation company centered on AI technology, operating under the mission that "AI should be accessible, practical, and empowering for everyone."

## Core Philosophy

AIがすべての人にとって身近で、実用的で、力を与える存在であるべきだと信じています。

Create a society where the value of AI can be experienced instantly and universally. Positioning AI at the center of creativity and collaboration between humans and intelligent systems.

## Business Divisions

### 1. InCosmos Art
Co-creation platform collaborating with artists worldwide using AI tools like Midjourney. Distributes AI-generated wallpapers and digital artworks through platforms including Dejavu Wallpaper, BoringDay.app, and Gumroad.

### 2. InCosmos Vision
Daily quick reads, reports, and private feeds providing AI-powered news and research with personalized access to financial and industry insights.

### 3. InCosmos AI Generation (StudioGen)
Intelligent marketing and operations system offering portraits, on-model looks, product shots, and social assets without heavy production requirements.

## Ecosystem Partners

- **Dejavu** — Wallpaper distribution network
- **iuufu.art** — Design-to-merch platform connecting manufacturers
- **ARTTOO** — Family jewelry brand collaborations
- **Magic Light Studio** — Partner studio for AI portrait generation
