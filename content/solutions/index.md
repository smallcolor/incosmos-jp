## FAQs

### Q: Under bulk licensing, can I use artworks commercially or resell them?
Commercial use on your own channels included. Resale/redistribution not included by default, but Merchandise/Resale Add‑on available.

**Allowed uses:** brand's web/app/social, in‑store screens, device UI, presentations, ads within agreed channels/territories.
**Not allowed:** reselling files, passing to third parties, stock uploads, sublicensing.
Physical product sales require Merchandise/Resale Add‑on with clear run/region terms.

### Q: How is bulk licensing different from a "buyout"?
- **Bulk licensing:** non‑exclusive, defined channels/territories/terms, no resale, fast delivery
- **Buyout‑style exclusive:** exclusive use for agreed scope, longer terms, broader usage
- **IP transfer (full copyright):** rare, custom works only
- **Hybrid option available:** start with bulk licensing, upgrade to exclusive later

### Q: Can you create custom AI artworks?
Yes. Process: brief → moodboard & sample set → style lock → production → delivery.
Deliverables: hi‑res JPG/PNG (4K+), required ratios, optional layered PSD.
Rights include license to channels; optional exclusivity or extended rights by request.

### Q: Can selected artworks become physical products?
Two routes:
1. License‑and‑manufacture (you supply files to vendor)
2. Co‑create via iuufu/Arttoo brands or deliver specs to your factory

Color matching varies by material; you handle product safety and compliance.

### Q: Can you design outfit looks and marketing assets for shoe sellers?
Yes—via StudioGen. We deliver on‑model looks, packshots, lifestyle scenes, and social‑ready short clips matching brand guide.

Require product photos/3D, brand guide, target channels, references, timelines.
Rights licensed to your channels; web/social included. We don't fabricate false fit/performance claims.

### Q: Can you produce custom research beyond vision reports?
Yes—through Vision Access for Teams.
Scope: ecommerce benchmarking, category/market scans, consumer journeys, channel economics, creative/testing playbooks.
Process: define questions/regions → agree sources/methods → draft → review → final pack.
Internal use with attribution; external publishing requires license.
