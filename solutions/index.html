Solution 2: StudioGen by InCosmos

AI generation for studios and ecommerce

On-brand portraits and product visuals without heavy shoots. Built for photography studios (white-label) and ecommerce teams.

Modes

  • PeopleGen — portraits, on-model looks
  • ProductGen — packshots, lifestyle comps
  • SocialGen — short clips

Workflow

  1. Intake (brand guide, SKUs/models, channels)
  2. Style lock (small sample set for approval)
  3. Batch generation (variants & review loops)
  4. Final delivery (DAM/Drive/S3, naming rules)

Deliverables

  • PNG/JPG 4K+
  • Masks & cutouts
  • Layered PSD on request
  • Multiple aspect ratios: 9:16 / 1:1 / 16:9
  • Optional LUT/style presets

Required Inputs

Product photos or 3D, brand guide, references, timelines; for portraits, consent/model releases

Usage Rights

Licensed to your channels. Web/social included by default; print/OOH/resale on request.
